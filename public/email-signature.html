How to install in Outlook (M365):
1. Select everything below this box (Cmd-A inside the white box, or click + drag).
2. Copy (Cmd-C).
3. In Outlook web: Settings → Mail → Compose and reply → Email signature. Paste in.
4. Check "Automatically include my signature on new messages" and "on messages I reply to."
